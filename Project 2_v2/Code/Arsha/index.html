Courtside


The classic. When you get into the old ages of 22 and 23, this will slowly become your favorite bar. The drinks are cheap, its spacious with a nice patio, and the pizza by the slice will save your life when you're at that stage of drunk.

">



North End


While it is the newest bar in town, it's easily got some of my favorite drinks. The frozen margaritas have tons of delicious flavors, their seasonal drinks are really great, and they have great food to boot. Their new bowls are some of my favorites, especially as someone who can't have much gluten.

">

Stephens


Despite having the scariest basement to any bar in town, the top of stephens is one of the best in town. With one of the only larger places to dance if that's what you're looking for, and a nice outdoor seating area, you're bound to spend a little more time here than you wanted. Plus, it's my favorite place in town to get people Tidal Waves.

">

Pigskin


Whether you're here for the unique ping pong table or occasional cornhole game, pigskin has a great atmosphere and lots of space for you and all your friends. Even if you're not out to drink, the food here is some of the best at a bar in Athens, including great food specials and the Black Widow and Red Death, some of the best drinks in town.

">

Broneys


While it's not what it used to be, Bronies still has a great patio and indoor seating to end the night off on. The famous Champagne Slushies are a must, adn their deals throughout the week can't be beat, like Taco Tuesday, and the 2 bars you can easily bounce between.



The Map



Athens/Court Street


Being a small college town, you can think of Court Street as the "downtown" of the college town. Thrift stores, a theater, great food, and lots of apartments and anemities can be found running the entire length from Baker Center down to the old armory. For our bar crawl, Court Street provides more than enough. On court street alone there are roughly 12 bars on the main street, giving plenty of options (sometimes maybe a bit too many options).
